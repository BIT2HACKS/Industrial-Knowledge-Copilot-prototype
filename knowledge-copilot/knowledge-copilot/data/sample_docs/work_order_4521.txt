WORK ORDER #WO-4521
Equipment Tag: P-101A (Feed Pump, Centrifugal)
Location: Unit 3, Process Area B
Date Raised: 2025-03-14
Raised By: Anil Kumar, Shift Supervisor
Priority: High

DESCRIPTION OF ISSUE
Feed pump P-101A tripped on high vibration alarm during routine operation. Vibration
reading at bearing housing (outboard) recorded at 9.8 mm/s RMS, exceeding trip
setpoint of 8.5 mm/s. Pump was isolated and standby unit P-101B started per SOP-22.

MAINTENANCE ACTIONS TAKEN
1. Bearing housing opened for inspection. Outboard bearing (SKF 6310) showed signs
   of spalling on the outer race.
2. Shaft alignment checked with laser alignment tool - found 0.15 mm angular
   misalignment against coupling, above the 0.05 mm tolerance in the OEM manual.
3. Bearing replaced. Shaft realigned to within tolerance.
4. Vibration reading after repair: 2.1 mm/s RMS at same measurement point.

ROOT CAUSE
Coupling misalignment during last major overhaul (Work Order WO-3980, October 2024)
was not corrected within OEM tolerance, leading to accelerated bearing wear over
approximately 5 months of operation.

RECOMMENDATION
Update SOP-22 alignment checklist to require laser alignment verification sign-off
by a second technician before pump is returned to service after any coupling work.
Cross-reference: Inspection Report IR-2025-089 recommends the same control for
all Unit 3 rotating equipment.
